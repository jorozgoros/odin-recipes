Veggie Lasagna

We're going to prepare this lasagna, no animal has been hurt in the making of it

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Tomato sauce
  • Whatever veggies you have in your fridge
  • (Optional) Cheese
  1. Follow the instructions for the lasagna sheets
  2. Cut the veggies as small as you like them
  3. Cook the veggies until soft
  4. Before finishing cooking the veggies, add the tomato sauce, salt and any spicies you like
  5. Alternate between one layer of sheets and a thin layer of veggies until you finish either of them
  6. Pace the final layer of sheets and optionally place some cheese on top of it
  7. Bake until the top is at the desired brown color
